My Journey

Below unfolds the timeline of my journey – a glimpse into the story that shapes me.

  1. 2005 - 2015

    Grew Up in a City on Sumatra Island

    I spent 10 years in one of Sumatra's major cities, Pekanbaru. I interacted with people from diverse ethnic backgrounds, such as Minang, Batak, Malay, and even Javanese. I attended school in this city from Kindergarten through 4th grade of Elementary School.

  2. 2015 - 2023

    Grew and Learned in My Hometown

    When I reached 5th grade, I relocated to the Semarang district in Central Java. I had to adjust to a new environment, language, and social circles. I attended middle school in Ambarawa and continued to high school in Salatiga City.

  3. 2023 - now

    Studied in the neighboring province of Central Java, namely Yogyakarta.

    Pursuing higher education in Yogyakarta.
